Privacy Policy

Funny Trivia Game values your privacy. This page explains how your data is handled when you use our game.

Local Data Storage

Leaderboard and game progress are stored only in your browser using local storage. This means:

  • Your scores and leaderboard are not sent to any server or shared with other users.
  • Each device/browser has its own local leaderboard.
  • If you clear your browser data, your leaderboard will be erased.

Trivia API Usage

This game uses the OpenTDB API to retrieve trivia questions.
No personal data is sent to OpenTDB. The API is used only to fetch trivia questions; it does not store or track any information about you or your gameplay.

No Account or Tracking

You do not need to create an account to play. We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact us via email.
